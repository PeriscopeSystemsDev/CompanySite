About Us

We aim to revolutionize facility management with an innovative IoT camera system focused on improving waste management. Our product utilizes smart wireless cameras connected to Wi-Fi, sending real-time notifications to custodial staff when trash cans require attention.

Connect With Us

We are committed to addressing the nationwide staffing shortage in the facility management sector. By providing a smart solution for waste management, we contribute to increased operational efficiency and enhanced cleanliness.

  • As of 2022, there were over 127,000 unfilled custodial positions
  • 61% of university facility managers reported to face hiring struggles
  • We hope to help alleviate the stress faced by custodial managers who deal with understaffing

Our Approach

At the core of our solution is an innovative IoT camera sensor designed to help facility managers ensure trash cans are attended to efficiently. The system provides real-time insights into the fill levels of trash cans, streamlining custodial operations and ensuring cleanliness across large facilities.

Our IoT camera sensors can be discreetly installed under the brim of a trash can lid or positioned on a nearby wall or ceiling. These cameras capture images periodically, monitoring the trash level in each bin throughout the day.

  • Image Capture: The cameras take snapshots of the trash cans at set intervals, providing a clear view of the current trash level.
  • Machine Learning Evaluation: Each image is sent to our machine learning model, which has been trained to determine whether the trash can is full, half-full, or empty. The model evaluates the trash levels with high accuracy, ensuring timely alerts for custodial teams.
  • Instant Alerts: When the system detects that a trash can is full, it immediately sends a notification through our app. Custodians receive the alert on their phones, allowing them to respond quickly and efficiently, reducing overflow and maintaining a clean environment.

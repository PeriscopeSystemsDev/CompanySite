Use Cases

Explore how our IoT camera sensor can help various industries manage their waste collection more efficiently.

Universities

With sprawling campuses and a large volume of foot traffic, universities require efficient waste management solutions. Our system helps custodial teams monitor trash levels across multiple buildings and outdoor spaces, ensuring that facilities remain clean without unnecessary collection trips.

K-12 Schools

Schools need a hygienic environment for students and staff. By using our product, facility managers can keep track of trash cans in high-use areas like cafeterias and playgrounds, reducing the chance of overflow and maintaining a tidy, welcoming space for everyone.

Hospitals

Cleanliness is critical in hospitals. Our IoT sensors ensure that trash receptacles in patient rooms, common areas, and medical wings are promptly emptied, reducing the risk of contamination and enhancing overall hygiene standards.

Stadiums & Event Centers

With large crowds during events, stadiums generate significant waste in a short period. Our product provides real-time updates on trash levels, enabling facility managers to coordinate waste removal efficiently and keep public spaces clean during peak activity.

Malls

Malls see high daily traffic and require constant waste management. Our solution enables facility teams to stay informed of trash levels throughout the day, ensuring that shopping areas remain tidy and appealing to visitors.

Amusement Parks

Amusement parks cover large areas and have waste disposal needs in both high-traffic zones and remote spots. With our sensors, management teams can stay on top of trash collection, providing a clean and enjoyable experience for guests while optimizing waste collection routes.
